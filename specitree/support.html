SpeciTree Support

For assistance, email beginnersforbeginners@gmail.com. Include your platform, SpeciTree version, and a description of the issue. Never email a backup password, Microsoft token, purchase token, or confidential laboratory data.

Back up your catalogue

  1. Open SpeciTree and go to Settings → Backup and restore.
  2. Confirm or change the local backup folder.
  3. Choose a filename ending in .labtrace.
  4. Optionally enter a password of at least eight characters. Passwords cannot be recovered.
  5. Keep the completion confirmation and test important backups.

Unencrypted backups can be read by anyone who obtains the file.

Restore safely

Choose Restore backup, select the .labtrace file, enter its password if required, and review the source, creation date, encryption state, and counts. Restore replaces the shared catalogue snapshot after an automatic safety backup. Do not interrupt the app or remove storage during restoration.

OneDrive

OneDrive is optional and requires a Plus subscription plus a user-owned Microsoft account. Disconnecting removes the local token but does not delete files already synchronized. Delete remote files separately through OneDrive.

Subscriptions

Purchases, renewal, cancellation, and payment methods are managed by Google Play. Use Settings → Subscriptions → Restore purchase after reinstalling or changing devices. To manage or cancel, open Google Play → profile picture → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → SpeciTree.

Data deletion

SpeciTree has no Beginners Lab online account and normally sends no catalogue records to Beginners Lab servers.

  • Delete records and photos using SpeciTree controls.
  • On Android, clear all remaining local data through Settings → Apps → SpeciTree → Storage → Clear storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Delete exported .labtrace files with the Files app or desktop file manager.
  • Disconnect OneDrive, then delete synchronized files through OneDrive.
  • Manage Google advertising data in Android privacy settings and through SpeciTree's Privacy choices button where displayed.
  • Manage Google Play purchase records and subscriptions through Google Play.

If you sent personal information directly to support, email beginnersforbeginners@gmail.com with “SpeciTree privacy request” in the subject. Do not send catalogue files or credentials.

Report a problem

Send the device model, Android or Windows version, SpeciTree version, the action you attempted, and the exact error message. Redact confidential details from screenshots.

Safety: SpeciTree is for general research inventory. Do not use it for patient records, clinical decisions, emergency response, or as the sole record required by a regulated quality system.
